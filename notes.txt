--- Sophisticated Investor Signal Stack ---

Per-stock fundamentals (in position detail panel):
- Forward P/E, PEG ratio, EV/EBITDA per holding
- Revenue growth YoY, gross margin %, profit margin %
- Analyst consensus price target vs current price ("expectations gap")
All available via yfinance.info

Analyst & sentiment signals (per holding):
- Analyst ratings distribution (Strong Buy → Strong Sell) — single bar
- Short interest % of float (high = crowded short or smart money warning)
- Insider transaction net direction last 90 days (yfinance.insider_transactions)

Technical / momentum signals:
- Price vs 50-day / 200-day moving average
- RSI (14-day) — overbought / oversold
- Volume trend vs 20-day average

Catalyst layer:
- Consensus EPS estimate for next earnings + beat/miss history
- Options market implied move for upcoming earnings

Portfolio-level "signals overview" table:
- All holdings side by side with color-coded columns per signal category
- Spot which positions have bull/bear alignment across multiple pillars

Core design idea: "Signal Stack" in position detail panel
  VALUATION | GROWTH | SENTIMENT
  Fwd P/E   | Rev %  | # Buy/Hold/Sell
  PEG       | Margin | Target vs price
  EV/EBITDA | EPS    | Short % / Insider
  + TECHNICALS row: RSI, MA position, volume
